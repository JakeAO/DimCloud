# Dark Cloud 1 — Story & Characters

## Protagonists

### Toan
- **Age**: ~14-15
- **Origin**: Norune Village
- **Role**: Atlamillia Wielder; Only Atla Collector
- **Weapon**: Sword/Dagger (Melee)
- **Personality**: Brave, Compassionate, Determined
- **Arc**: Village Boy → World Savior
- **Key Relationships**: Fairy King (Mentor), Xiao (Friend), Seda (Guide), Dran (Freed Guardian)

### Xiao
- **True Form**: Cat (Lily)
- **Transformed By**: Seda's "Changing Potion"
- **Weapon**: Slingshot (Ranged)
- **Ability**: **Jump** — Cross Gaps, Reach High Places
- **Personality**: Playful, Loyal, Cat-like Mannerisms
- **Arc**: Accepts Dual Nature (Human/Cat)
- **Key Moment**: Helps Toan Cross Divine Beast Cave Gaps

### Goro
- **Origin**: Matataki Village (Hunter)
- **Weapon**: Hammer/Axe (Melee, Heavy)
- **Ability**: **Break Rocks** — Cracked Walls, Boulders
- **Personality**: Proud, Strong, Protective
- **Arc**: Earns Battle Axe → Inferno Path
- **Joins**: Matataki Village 100% Requests

### Ruby
- **True Form**: Genie (Freed from Lamp)
- **Weapon**: Magic Ring (Ranged, Elemental)
- **Ability**: **Float** — Hover Over Gaps/Traps
- **Personality**: Vain, Loyal, Fish-out-of-Water
- **Arc**: Freedom → Humanity → Witch Parfait Reward
- **Joins**: Queens (Post-Shipwreck)

### Ungaga
- **Origin**: Muska Lacka (Desert Warrior)
- **Weapon**: Staff/Spear (Melee, Reach)
- **Ability**: **Sandwalk** — Quicksand Immunity
- **Personality**: Stoic, Dutiful, Warrior's Code
- **Arc**: Tribal Duty → Sun Giant Pilot
- **Joins**: Muska Lacka (Post-Sun/Moon Temple)

### Osmond
- **Origin**: Moon People Leader (Yellow Drops)
- **Weapon**: Gun/Blaster (Ranged, High DPS)
- **Ability**: **Unlock Doors** — Shoot Switches/Locks
- **Personality**: Noble, Scientific, Leader
- **Arc**: Restore Moon Magic → Sun Giant Crew
- **Joins**: Yellow Drops (Post-Moon Sea)

## Antagonists

### Dark Genie
- **Origin**: Seda's Grief + Dark Power
- **Released By**: Colonel Flag Gilgister
- **True Form**: Rat (Possessed) + Genie Energy
- **Goal**: Destroy World; Feed on Despair
- **Defeated By**: Sun Giant + Toan's Atlamillia

### Colonel Flag Gilgister
- **Role**: Lagoon Empire Army Officer
- **Motivation**: Power; Control Genie
- **Fate**: Possessed by Genie; Dies When Genie Destroyed
- **Significance**: Human Catalyst for Tragedy

### Seda (The Ancient King)
- **Origin**: Ruler 100+ Years Ago
- **Tragedy**: Lost Love (Sophia) → Accepted Dark Power
- **Created**: Dark Genie (Unintentionally)
- **Role in Game**: Guide; Gives Toan Changing Potion/Xiao
- **Redemption**: Toan Restores Sophia via Atlamillia

## Party Dynamics

### Traversal Puzzle Design
```
Divine Beast Cave:      Toan Only (Tutorial)
Wise Owl Forest:        Xiao Jump → Gaps
Shipwreck:              Ruby Float → Water Gaps
Sun & Moon Temple:      Ungaga Sandwalk → Quicksand
Moon Sea:               Osmond Door Shot → Locked Doors
Gallery of Time:        All Traversals Required
```

### Combat Synergy
| Situation | Best Character | Reason |
|-----------|----------------|--------|
| Flying Enemies | Xiao / Ruby | Ranged + Elemental |
| Armored Enemies | Goro (Smash Crystal) | High Smash Dmg |
| Undead Hordes | Ruby (Exorcism) / Toan (Holy) | Holy Dmg |
| Tight Corridors | Toan / Osmond | Melee / Ranged Flex |
| Boss DPS | Goro (Charge) / Osmond (Gun) | Burst Damage |

## Character Design Evolution (DC1 → DC2)

| DC1 Character | DC2 Spiritual Successor | Notes |
|---------------|-------------------------|-------|
| **Toan** (Sword) | **Max** (Wrench/Gun) + **Monica** (Sword) | Split Protagonist |
| **Xiao** (Slingshot/Jump) | **Monica** (Transform) + **Steve** (Movement) | Split |
| **Goro** (Hammer/Rocks) | **Max** (Steve Legs/Arms) + **Monica** (Transform) | Merged into Systems |
| **Ruby** (Ring/Fly) | **Monica** (Armband/Magic) + **Steve** (Hover) | Split |
| **Ungaga** (Staff/Sandwalk) | **Monica** (Transform: Aquatic/Reptile) | Transform System |
| **Osmond** (Gun/Doors) | **Max** (Gun) + **Steve** (Radar/Keys) | Split |

> **Design Philosophy**: DC2 "Unbundled" the 6 single-purpose characters into 2 deep protagonists + modular systems (Steve, Transform, Support NPCs).
