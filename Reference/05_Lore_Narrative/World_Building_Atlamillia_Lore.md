# Dark Cloud Series — Atlamillia & World Lore

## The Atlamillia: Living Stones

### Definition
**Atlamillia** (アトラミリア) — Sentient Jewels of Ancient Origin. Three exist. Each chooses its wielder. They are the keys to Time, Space, and World Restoration.

---

## Three Atlamillia

| Atlamillia | Color | Power | Known Wielder | Game |
|------------|-------|-------|---------------|------|
| **Earth Atlamillia** | Green | Travel 100 Years **Future** | Max (DC2) | DC2 |
| **Moon Atlamillia** | Blue | Travel 100 Years **Past** | Monica (DC2) | DC2 |
| **Sun Atlamillia** | Gold/Red | Unknown (Plot Key) | — | DC2 |
| **Atlamillia (Single)** | Multi | Atla Absorption/Placement; Soul Recall | Toan (DC1) | DC1 |

### Shared Abilities (All Atlamillia)
| Ability | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Wielder Selection** | Stone Chooses; Cannot Be Forced/Stolen |
| **Atla/Geostone Resonance** | Detects & Absorbs Atla Spheres / Geostones |
| **Essence Storage** | Infinite Capacity for World Pieces (Atla/Blueprints) |
| **Manifestation** | Converts Stored Essence → Physical Objects in 3D Space |
| **Soul Interaction** | Can Recall Wandering Spirits (Cost: Atlamillia Power) |

## DC1: The Atlamillia (Single Stone)

### Origin
- **Giver**: Fairy King Simba
- **Recipient**: Toan (Chosen by Stone)
- **Purpose**: Restore World Sealed by Fairy King

### Unique Abilities
| Ability | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Atla Absorption** | Only Toan Can Touch/Collect Atla Spheres |
| **Georama Placement** | Manifest Atla Pieces in Real-Time 3D |
| **Soul Recall** | Used Once: Resurrected Sophia (Consumed Power) |
| **Atlamillia Sword** | Final Weapon Form (Atlamillia + Macho Sword Evolution) |

### Limitations
- **Single Wielder** (Toan Only)
- **No Time Travel** (DC1 Lore: Time Not Traversable)
- **Soul Recall = Power Drain** (One-Time Use)

## Atlamillia Lore Connections

### DC1 → DC2 Continuity
| DC1 Element | DC2 Expansion |
|-------------|---------------|
| Single Atlamillia | **Three Atlamillia** (Time/Space Trinity) |
| Fairy King Gift | **Ancient Civilization Creation** |
| Toan's Soul Recall | **Moon/Sun Badges** (Meta-Transformation) |
| Atlamillia Sword | **Atlamillia Weapons** (Ultimate Evolution Paths) |
| Atla Spheres | **Geostones** (Data → Blueprints) |

### The Ancient Civilization (10,000+ Years Ago)
- **Creators**: Built Atlamillia as Tools of Order
- **Purpose**: Maintain Time/Space Balance
- **Fall**: Great War → Dark Power → Genie Born
- **Survivors**: Moon People (Fled to Moon); Fairy King (Spirit Realm)
- **Legacy**: Atlamillia, Sun Giant/Paznos, Moon Flower Palace

## Atlamillia & The Dark Genie/Griffon

### DC1: Genie vs Atlamillia
- **Genie**: Destruction; Chaos; Negative Emotion Manifest
- **Atlamillia**: Preservation; Order; Restoration
- **Climax**: Atlamillia Sword + Sun Giant = Genie Destroyed

- **Griffon**: Time Corruption; Erasure; Entropy
- **Atlamillia (3)**: Time Mastery; Causality; Hope
