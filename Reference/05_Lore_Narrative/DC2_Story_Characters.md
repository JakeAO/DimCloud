# Dark Cloud 2 — Story & Characters

## Dual Protagonists

### Maximilian (Max)
- **Age**: ~15-16
- **Origin**: Palm Brinks (Present Era)
- **Background**: Inventor Apprentice; Son of Gerald (Legendary Inventor)
- **Weapon Loadout**:
  - **Right Hand (Melee)**: Wrench / Hammer / Sword / Drill
  - **Left Hand (Ranged)**: Gun / Cannon / Laser / Missile
  - **Ridepod (Steve)**: Modular Mech (Separate XP/SP/WHP)
- **Special**: **Camera** → Photography → Invention
- **Atlamillia**: **Earth** → Travel 100 Years Future
- **Personality**: Curious, Optimistic, Mechanical Genius
- **Arc**: Apprentice → Master Inventor → Timeline Guardian
- **Father**: Gerald (Recruitable Support: +1 SP/Level for Guns)

### Monica Raybrandt
- **Age**: ~15-16
- **Origin**: Future Era (100 Years Ahead)
- **Background**: Princess of Future Kingdom; Father Killed by Griffon
- **Weapon Loadout**:
  - **Right Hand (Melee)**: Sword / Katana
  - **Left Hand (Ranged)**: Armband (Magic: Fire/Ice/Lightning/Wind/Holy)
  - **Monster Transformation**: 12 Badges → Forms
- **Atlamillia**: **Moon** → Travel 100 Years Past
- **Personality**: Determined, Noble, Vengeful → Protective
- **Arc**: Revenge → Duty → Sacrifice → Hope
- **Key Relationship**: Max (Partner), Father (Motivation), Lin (Mentor)

## Ridepod — Steve
- **Nature**: AI Mech Companion (Not a Character)
- **Customization**: Body / Legs / Arms / Melee Weapon / Ranged Weapon
- **Fuel**: Energy Pack (Invented Ch.1) → Consumed in Combat
- **XP/SP/WHP**: Independent from Max/Monica
- **Mount/Dismount**: Real-Time (R-Stick Click)
- **Evolution**: Parts Spectrumize → Transfer Stats
- **Ultimate**: Sun & Moon Armor + Jet Hover + Ultimate Cannon

## Antagonists

### Emperor Griffon
- **Origin**: Future Era Ruler; Corrupted by Dark Power
- **Goal**: Erase Origin Points → Rewrite History
- **Method**: Destroy Origin Points in Past → Future Collapses
- **True Form**: Dark Entity (Post-Paznos Battle)
- **Connection to DC1**: Spiritual Successor to Dark Genie (Time Corruption)
- **Defeat**: Paznos Summoned → Chronos Union → True Form Destroyed

### Gaspard / Gargoyle
- **Role**: Griffon's Right Hand; Enforcer
- **Fate**: Defeated; Reveals Griffon's Plan

### La Saia (Returning)
- **Role**: Sand Clone Boss (Starlight Canyon)
- **Connection**: DC1 La Saia → Future Version

## Character Design Philosophy (Hino)

> "In Dark Cloud 1, we had six characters each with one purpose. In Dark Cloud 2, we wanted two characters who could do everything — but through systems, not stats. Max builds. Monica transforms. Steve is their third arm. The NPCs are passive skills you equip by building the world. The party is the world."
